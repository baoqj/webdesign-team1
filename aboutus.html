Brand Story


Thé Bora was established in 2012, Montreal. With many years of skills and expertise developed by founder Jane Kim, Thé Bora became one of the Montreal's fastest growing bubble tea stores, offering high quality beverages.

Thé Bora ensures that all drinks are served at their freshest by brewing new batches of tea and pearls every four hours. Also, Thé Bora offers a wide selection of the very best toppings, including pearls and various jellies, to pair with these teas.

This commitment to excellence ensures that Thé Bora is always brewing happiness wherever we serve our customers.
