Hong Kong Bubble Tea

Hong Kong bubble tea is an iced milk tea made with black tea, tapioca balls, and sweetened condensed milk or evaporated milk instead of regular milk. The boba, or the tapioca balls in the drink are makes this beverage a bubble tea. Take out the boba and it’s a Hong Kong-style milk tea.
